JORYBORD

Scroll down for tutorial



Click and drag to make a selection. Selections must be at least 3 letters long.

Click to change a single letter in your selection and turn it into a valid word.

Each letter in your word will give you a number of points equal to
the length of the word, so the word "BOTH" here is worth 16 points.

Letters can only be modified once, so we couldn't turn this 'B' into an
'M' to spell "MOM", but we could turn the 'M' into an 'O' to spell "BOO"

You can only get points from each letter once, so the word "BOO" here only gives 6
points instead of 9.The color under a letter tells you how many points it was worth.

Try to set yourself up to spell long words for big points!

When you fill the whole grid or decide you're out of moves, you
can see your final score and share how you did with your friends!
